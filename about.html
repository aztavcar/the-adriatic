Follow the current, not the noise.

The Adriatic is the publishing arm of the Institute for Strategic Solutions, a Ljubljana-based consultancy that has worked with businesses across the region since 2011. What began as Strategic Foresight - a bulletin tracking shifts in politics and markets - has grown into a publication for readers who want to understand the currents shaping the Western Balkans and the world beyond.

We publish essays, reportage and analysis on politics, business and culture. New work appears online throughout the year; an annual print edition collects each year's essential reading into a single volume worth keeping.

Contribute

We publish essays, reportage, and criticism on politics, business, and culture. A piece on port logistics in Koper might sit alongside an essay on American decline or a profile of a Croatian architect. What connects the work is clarity and a perspective rooted in this region.

We welcome submissions from journalists, academics, analysts, and writers who have something to say. We're interested in work that wouldn't find a home elsewhere - too specific for global publications, too ambitious for local press.

Submissions

Send completed drafts of at least 500 words to info@isr.si. Include a brief note on who you are and why the piece belongs here. We read everything.
