Design notes

  • Container: Drops the gradient background + white border + inset glow in favor of a flat rgba(255,255,255,0.85) with a single 0.5px ring — same language as the like button and view-more chips
  • Shadow: Replaces the 4-layer shadow (including inset highlights) with a cleaner 3-layer stack — ring + medium spread + soft ambient. No inset tricks.
  • Border-radius: 16px → 10px. Tighter, matching the 6px chip radius proportionally.
  • Nav links: B/C use monospace at 0.6875rem with opacity-based color (0.45 default → 0.8 hover). D keeps serif (Adriatic22) for editorial warmth, monospace only for the CTA.
  • Active state: Replaces font-weight: 600 with a subtle background chip (rgba(0,0,0,0.04)). Weight stays at 400 — consistent with agentation's "state through tint, not weight" rule.
  • Subscribe button: B is fully neutral (gray chip). C/D use accent-red tint — same treatment as the liked state on the like button (rgba(189,45,45,0.04) bg + accent ring). Gives it just enough CTA energy without the gradient.
  • Arrow dot: Replaces the animated double-chevron pill with a simple 4px accent dot. Quieter, but signals "there's something here." Dot brightens on hover.
  • Home icon: Drops from 20px to 18px, color from solid text to rgba(0,0,0,0.35). Recedes into the background until hovered.
