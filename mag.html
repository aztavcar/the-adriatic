Each annual edition of The Adriatic features original cover art by Ciril Horjak, the Slovenian illustrator and comic strip artist who received the Prešeren Award - Slovenia's highest recognition for artistic achievement. We commission new work for every issue, transforming each edition into something between magazine and art book. A collectible object designed to hold its place on the shelf long after the news cycle has moved on.

The print magazine launches each January at our flagship Strategic Foresight gathering in Ljubljana, where business leaders, policymakers and intellectuals convene to discuss the forces shaping the region's future. To secure your copy and stay informed about upcoming events, sign up to our newsletter.
